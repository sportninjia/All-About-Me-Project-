Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Adarsh Rajesh! I’m passionate about exploring the world of business and technology, and I enjoy working on creative projects that let me express my skills and ideas. Outside of academics, I have a deep love for dance, instrumental performances, and gaming. I even run a YouTube channel where I share these interests with others. It’s my way of connecting with people who share the same enthusiasm for creativity and entertainment. When I’m not busy with schoolwork or creating content, I’m likely practicing Kalari, a martial art I’ve been learning for a few months. It's a great way for me to stay active and disciplined. I’ve also achieved milestones that I’m proud of, like receiving an award from the Iowa State Senate for being a good student. I value hard work and perseverance and strive to improve myself every day. One of my dreams is to make an impact in the business world while continuing to embrace my hobbies and passions. Life’s journey is an exciting one, and I’m determined to make the most of it!
